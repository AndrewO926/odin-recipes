Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

In this recipe, you will be mixing butter, sugar, eggs, flour, and decadent chocolate chips to make the best cookies to ever grace your lips.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Sugar
  • Chocolate Chips
  • Vanilla
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Butter
  • Brown sugar

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375 degree fahrenheit
  2. Cream together butter, sugars, vanilla, and eggs
  3. In a separate bowl, mix together dry ingredients
  4. Slowly mix together dry and wet in a standmixer
  5. Grease a baking tray or lay parchment paper down on it
  6. Portion out cookies by about two to three tablespoons batter each
  7. Bake each batch for about 8 minutes or until the bottoms are just golden
  8. Let cookies rest for at least 10 minutes after baking
  9. Enjoy!
