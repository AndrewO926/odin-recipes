Pumpkin Bread

Description

This scrumptuous pumpkin bread will have your mouth watering before it even makes it out of the oven. A distasterously, delicious combination of sugar, flour, and pumpkin will have you craving this everyday of your life. A perfect thing to pair with tea or coffee in the morning, or to just slather with some butter and eat by itself

Ingredients

  • Canned pumpkin
  • Sugar
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • Baking Soda
  • Salt
  • Eggs
  • Cinnamon
  • Pumpkin Spice

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degree farhenheit
  2. Mix together pumpkin, sugar, and eggs in the bowl of a stand mixer
  3. In a separate bowl, mix your dry ingredients together
  4. Combine both bowls together in the stand mixer, mixing slowly at first
  5. Grease the inside of two loaf tins
  6. Pour half your mixture into each tin
  7. Use a spatula to even out the tops of both loaves
  8. Pop both in the oven and let bake for 50 minutes
  9. Remove from oven and let sit for five minutes
  10. Turn over both loafes onto a wire rack to let cool completely
  11. Once completely cooled, cut in and enjoy!
