Decadent Peanut Butter Chocolate Oatmeal

Description

The best way to start of your day with healthy, delicious oatmeal. That will keep you full and energized throughout your morning.

Ingredients

  • Oatmeal (old fashioned or quick)
  • Sweetener of choice
  • Peanut Butter
  • Cocoa powder
  • Peanut Butter Powder
  • Sugar free chocolate jello mix
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix together all ingredients except for cinnamon and peanut butter
  2. Put your bowl in the microwave and cook for two and a half minutes
  3. Spread peanut butter on top of your oatmeal and dust it with cinnamon
  4. Enjoy!
